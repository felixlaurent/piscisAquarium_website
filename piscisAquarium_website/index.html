Introduction

Located in Jakarta, Piscis Aquarium is one

of the largest and most spectacular aquarium

in Indonesia. At Piscis Aquarium, we have more than

25000 aquatic animals with over 10 large tanks, taking

visitors on a virtual tour of the Oceans. With guindance

of our professional staff, you and your family at all ages

will get exciting information about the aquatic animals.

You can also feel a closer interaction with some of our marine animals in the

interactive exhibition area, a space created to present a new experience

in interacting with the underwater world, including touching sharks and starfish.

Dive deep into our gorgeous aquatic realm and get ready

to be enthralled by a world you've never seen before!
